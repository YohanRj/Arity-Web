Enhancing STEM Education in Sri Lanka through VR and Chatbot Integration

The existing gap in effective Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) education in Sri Lanka, marked by disengagement and lack of personalized learning, prompts the exploration of innovative solutions like virtual reality (VR) and chatbot technologies. This study aims to address these educational challenges and bridge the gap by integrating VR-based immersive experiences and an intelligent chatbot to elevate the quality and engagement of STEM education in the Sri Lankan context.

Revolutionizing STEM Education: VR and Chatbot Integration

Leveraging the power of virtual reality (VR) and intelligent chatbots, our solution aims to transform STEM education in Sri Lanka. By integrating immersive VR experiences and interactive chatbot assistance, we propose a holistic approach to enhance student engagement, understanding, and overall performance in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics subjects.

Strategic Implementation for STEM Learning Advancement

Our methodology involves the seamless integration of VR technology and chatbot assistance into the educational framework. The process includes the development of VR-enhanced quizzes, challenges, and a virtual classroom, alongside the implementation of a disease prediction system. User profiles and recommendations will be personalized, ensuring an adaptive and engaging learning journey for each student. Rigorous testing, refinement, and comprehensive data analysis will guide the iterative enhancement of the system for optimal educational impact.
